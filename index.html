AlgoBridge
Algorithm + Bridge — bridging the gap between algorithms and every student's future.

Witnessing how DOT Glasses transformed lives in marginalized communities with simple clear vision, I saw a parallel: digital literacy is the clear vision of the 21st century. Millions of students graduate without ever understanding algorithms or AI — the engines powering the modern world. So I built AlgoBridge, running four interactive workshops for students aged 11–18 in underserved schools. No jargon, no code, no prior knowledge required. Just curiosity — and the belief that every student deserves to understand the technology shaping their world.

✓
High engagement and active participation from all students across every session
✓

✓

What is Coding & Debugging?
